Data Deletion

Orderly Foods · Last updated: July 14, 2026

You can request deletion of personal data associated with Orderly Foods Services (ordering sites, mobile apps, and related tools). This page also serves as the Meta / Facebook data deletion instructions for Orderly apps that connect to Facebook or Instagram.

How to request deletion

  1. Email privacy@orderlyfoods.com with subject line Data deletion request.
  2. Include: your name, phone/email used for orders, restaurant/app name (if known), and Facebook/Instagram user ID if the request relates to a social login or messaging feature.
  3. We will confirm receipt and complete deletion or anonymization of personal data we control within 30 days, unless retention is required by law (e.g. tax records).

What we delete or anonymize

  • Account profile fields and contact details we store for you.
  • Push tokens and optional marketing preferences.
  • Social inbox message content tied to your identity where we are the processor for a connected Page (subject to restaurant record-keeping needs).

What we may retain

  • Transaction records required for accounting, fraud prevention, or legal compliance (often retained in minimized/anonymized form).
  • Data held solely by the restaurant or payment processor (Square) — you may also need to contact them.

Meta app users

If you used a Facebook Login or messaging feature powered by an Orderly Meta app, submit the email request above. We will delete data received from Meta associated with your user identifier, then email confirmation when done.
