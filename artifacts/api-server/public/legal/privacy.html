Privacy Policy

Orderly Foods · Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Orderly Foods (“Orderly”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our restaurant ordering websites, mobile apps, dashboards, and related services (the “Services”), including white-label apps operated for restaurant partners such as Samurai Hibachi & Sushi.

1. Information we collect

  • Order details: items ordered, special instructions, tip amounts, pickup preferences, and order status.
  • Contact details: name, phone number, and email when you provide them for an order or account.
  • Payment information: card payments are processed by our payment partners (for example Square). We do not store full card numbers on Orderly servers.
  • Device & usage: app/browser type, approximate location (if permitted), push notification tokens, and basic analytics needed to run the Services.
  • Social messaging (optional products): if a restaurant enables Meta inbox tools, we may receive public Page comments/messages the restaurant chooses to connect, solely to help staff draft or send human-approved replies.

2. How we use information

  • To place and fulfill pickup/orders with the restaurant you selected.
  • To send transactional messages (order confirmation, ready-for-pickup notifications).
  • To operate restaurant owner tools (dashboard, menu, order status).
  • To improve reliability, prevent fraud/abuse, and comply with law.
  • We do not sell personal information.

3. Sharing

We share information with:

  • The restaurant fulfilling your order (merchant of record for food service).
  • Payment processors and POS providers (e.g. Square) as needed to charge and reconcile orders.
  • Infrastructure providers that host the Services.
  • Platforms you connect (e.g. Meta) when a restaurant enables those integrations.

4. Retention

We keep order and account records as long as needed to provide the Services, meet legal/accounting requirements, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of account-linked personal data as described in our Data Deletion page.

5. Your choices

  • Decline optional permissions (e.g. push notifications) in your device settings.
  • Contact the restaurant for order-specific questions; contact Orderly for platform privacy requests.

6. Children

The Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
